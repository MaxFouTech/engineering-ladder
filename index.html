The Engineering Ladder

Most engineering ladders still reward the things AI is getting good at. Code velocity. Feature throughput. Sprint completion. The things that actually make an engineer irreplaceable — judgment about what to build, instinct for what will break, the ability to translate a technical risk into a business cost — get no column in the spreadsheet.

This tool evaluates engineers against a five-rung ladder — from Apprentice to Strategist — scoring observable signals across three categories at each level. Concrete signals a manager can actually point to in a conversation.

Based on "The CTO's New Engineering Ladder" by Etienne de Bruin, March 2026
